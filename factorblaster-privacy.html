PRIVACY POLICY

FACTOR BLASTER
Last updated: May 2026

Factor Blaster does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Period.

WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT

The app does not require an account or login. We do not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any other personal data. We do not use analytics, third-party tracking, or advertising SDKs.

YOUR GAME DATA

All game data — your score, streak, and level progress — exists only on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, never shared with any third party, and never used for any purpose beyond displaying it to you during your session.

CHILDREN

Factor Blaster is designed for students of all ages including children under 13. Because we collect no data whatsoever, the app is fully compliant with COPPA and FERPA.

CHANGES

If this policy ever changes, we will update the date above. Given that we collect no data, significant changes are unlikely.
