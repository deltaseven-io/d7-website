FAQ

WHAT MATH TOPICS DOES THE GAME COVER?

Factor Blaster covers GCF factoring, Difference of Two Squares, and Trinomial factoring with leading coefficients of 1, prime values, and composite values. All topics found in a standard Algebra 1 curriculum.

WHAT IS EZ MODE VS CHALLENGE MODE?

EZ Mode shows four multiple choice answers — tap the correct factored form. Challenge Mode removes the choices and lets you spin number wheels to build the answer yourself. Challenge Mode is significantly harder and better for exam prep.

HOW MANY LEVELS ARE THERE?

Six levels. Each level requires 5 correct answers to advance. Complete all six and you win. A bonus heart is awarded at the end of each level.

DOES THE APP SAVE MY PROGRESS?

Score and streak are tracked within each session. The game resets when you start a new game. There are no accounts or cloud saves.

IS THIS APP FREE?

Yes. Factor Blaster is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

WHAT GRADES IS THIS FOR?

Factor Blaster is designed for students in Algebra 1, typically grades 8–10. It's also useful for anyone brushing up on factoring skills before a standardized test.
